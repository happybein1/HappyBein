Our Initiatives

Five active instruments in the HappyBein ecosystem — each one a focused tool designed to help individuals simplify complexity, build leverage, and live with more purpose.

⚡
YeahVibe
yeahvibe.com
Core Platform
"Stop managing time. Start building leverage."

The remedy for modern chaos — the overwhelm, the paralysis, the feeling of being drained while standing still. YeahVibe pre-filters the noise into a prioritised Master List for high-leverage living, structured around 5 Pillars that cover every dimension of a well-directed life.

The 5 Pillars of Leverage
  • 1Direction & Priorities — Define your North Star so every effort counts.
  • 2Mindset — Cognitive reframing and resilience for sustainable growth.
  • 3Surroundings & Processes — Reduce friction, reclaim energy.
  • 4Collaboration & Leadership — Scale your impact through others.
  • 5YeahSight — Understand the world to navigate it confidently.

YeahSight
yeahsight.com
World Context
"To win, you must see the board."

Pillar 5 of the YeahVibe framework — the external lens. The remedy for feeling concerned about the world's future. Efficiency without direction is aimless; YeahSight decodes global trends, geopolitical shifts, and societal forces into plain language so you can define a confident, long-term direction.

  • Global trends translated into actionable insight
  • Systemic understanding of how the world impacts daily decisions
  • Moves you from Confused → Informed → Directed

YeahVibe Workout
workout.yeahvibe.com
Micro-Exercise Tool
"Move a little. Gain a lot."

A lightweight tool that randomly selects short exercises suited for home or work situations. No gym required — just a quick, randomised movement break to counter sedentary routines. Each exercise comes with a how-to video so you always know exactly what to do.

  • Random exercise selection — home & office friendly
  • Instructional video for every exercise
  • FR / EN — available in French and English
  • "Next Exercise" flow keeps sessions fast and frictionless

YeahVibe GameTrack
gametrack.yeahvibe.com
Score Tracker
"Track the score. Raise the stakes."

A simple, focused score tracker for games. Whether you are playing board games, card games, or any activity that needs a live scoreboard, GameTrack keeps the count clear so you can focus on playing — not remembering numbers.

  • Real-time score tracking for any game format
  • Lightweight and distraction-free — built for play
  • Works for multiplayer sessions — family, friends, or teams
