Six instruments in the HappyBein ecosystem — each one a focused tool designed to help individuals simplify complexity, build leverage, and live with more purpose.

HappyBein Foundation
happybein.com
The Architect

A purpose-driven foundation dedicated to simplifying societal complexity. We build the frameworks that help individuals and leaders navigate a noisy world with clarity.

  • Research studio studying societal challenges and curating best practices
  • Defines the vision and answers "Why does this matter?"
  • Non-profit, Eindhoven, Netherlands — founded 2021
  • Operates across two streams: Internal Efficiency + External Understanding

YeahVibe
yeahvibe.com
Core Platform

The remedy for modern chaos. A free, modular repository of "Life Recipes" structured around 5 Pillars of Leverage — turning abstract wisdom into clear, actionable steps.

The 5 Pillars of Leverage
  • 1Direction & Priorities — Define your North Star so every effort counts.
  • 2Mindset — Cognitive reframing for sustainable growth.
  • 3Surroundings & Processes — Reduce friction, reclaim energy.
  • 4Collaboration & Leadership — Scale your impact through others.
  • 5YeahSight — Understand the world to navigate it confidently.

SpeakNote
speaknote.happybein.com
Voice Transcription

Listens to what you say — in any language — and writes it down instantly. Your data stays private and local, ready to copy to clipboard whenever you need it.

"Speak it. Store it. Use it."

  • Real-time speech-to-text across multiple languages
  • 100% local — no data sent to any server, ever
  • One-tap export to clipboard for use anywhere
  • No account, no signup, no tracking
